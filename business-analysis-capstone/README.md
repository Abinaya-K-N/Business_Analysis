## Project Overview

This project presents a complete **end-to-end business data analysis workflow** developed as part of a Data Analytics Internship Capstone. The objective is to transform raw business datasets into actionable insights using data analysis and visualization techniques.

The project integrates analysis across three business domains:

- Sales Performance Analysis
- Customer Churn Analysis
- Housing Market Price Analysis

---

## Business Objectives

- Perform structured data cleaning and preprocessing
- Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Generate professional visualizations
- Calculate key business KPIs
- Identify patterns affecting business outcomes
- Provide data-driven recommendations
- Build a reproducible analytics pipeline

## Key Business Insights

### Sales
- Revenue concentrated in mid-value transactions
- Regional differences influence performance

### Customer Churn
- Low overall churn rate
- Contract type impacts retention

### Housing Market
- Property area strongly correlates with price
- Market dominated by mid-price properties
